FEATURES

Shacli creates a shared clipboard between your PC and its clients, allowing you to share anything and everything with ease.

Simple and modern interface

Shacli aims to save you time, not waste you time, which is why the interface is very minimalist and modern.

No internet access required

Just connect your devices in the same local network and you are ready to use Shacli (you can also use a wifi hotpost).

Multi-format support

You can send absolutely anything through Shacli. Files, html links and even text.

Good user experience

Sharing data with Shaco is as easy and intuitive as copying and pasting.
